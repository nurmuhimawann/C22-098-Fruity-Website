Our Mission

Everyone is interested in getting fresh and quality fruits, starting from farmers, fruit sellers, and industry to consumers. As fruits are going to rot with time. Fruit quality has a major economic and health impact. The presence of rotten fruit can cause huge financial losses. In addition, fruit sales will be affected because consumers consider rotten fruit harmful to health. Manual classification of fresh and rotten fruit is ineffective. High productivity at low cost is the hope for fruit growers, sellers and the fruit processing industry. And fresh fruit and safe for consumption are the hope for consumers. Therefore, Fruity is here as a utility that can help you increase your productivity to find rotten fruit quickly so you get fresh fruit that is safe for consumption.
